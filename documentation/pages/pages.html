· Multi page articles
Over View: Ncaster features a very powerful multi page system, each article can contain it's own set of linked pages ncaster goes beyond this and allows each of those linked pages to have there own pages also.

Tip: Multi page articles are slower then a normal page to load but not by a large amount, ncaster makes an optimization for pages with no assigned pages.

Note: note the multi page system supported in ncaster 1.6.5 is no longer supported because of comparability reasons, this new system is both faster and uses no additional query's, You cannot use multi page articles with in a relational link (rid=id) for the time being.

- Adding a page to your new article.
- Deleting & Editing a mutlipage article.

Adding a page to your article.

There is currently two ways to add a new page to your article, when you post an article you are given a option to add a new page or you can simple click add new page from the edit screen.

Deleting & editing.

Editing & deleting multipage articles is quite easy, all pages that are linked together are grouped together on the edit screen from here you can delete or edit any article including multipage articles.
