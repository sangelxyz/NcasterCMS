Categorys .

In this section we will cover.
* Creating categorys
* Editing categorys.
* Deleting categorys.

Category Creation.
With ncaster you have the ability to add categorys to seperate data, ncaster 1.7 has some nice new editions such as category avatar's and now the ability to assign a template when creating or editing a category to make life just that bit easier.

Creating Categorys :
To create a category simply login to your admin click on settings > add a category.

Editing Categorys :
To edit a category simply login to your admin click on settings > edit a category, next you will given a list of possible category's to edit simply click the category you wish to edit.
Note: In ncaster 1.7 if you change a category name you will lose all previous posts in the new category name but the posts them self's will still exists under the old category name, in future versions this will be addressed.

Deleting Categorys :
To Delete a category simply login to your admin click on settings > remove a category.
Note: Deleting a category will not remove any data.
