· Article Authentication
Over View: As of ncaster 1.7.2, ncaster now supports article authentication this allows you to edit, delete, move news items before they are published to be viewed by your readers.

Only two groups can currently edit non-published news items and they are admin and the new editor group, the editor group is almost the same as the admin with out the ability to change any settings, but he can edit any article. To enable the article authentication system go to settings > display settings and enable it.
